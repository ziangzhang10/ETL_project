NBA Player Salary Study

What Determines the Salaries of NBA Players?

Yang Yang and ZiAng Zhang

Background of Study

The purpose of our project is to identify factors that may determine the income of NBA players, such as age, height, position in the court, field goal percentage, etc, and analyze their correlations. Stronger correlation may indicate a possible causation. Please see the figures on this page with our analysis. You're also welcome to draw your own conclusions.

Our observations:

  • Overall, the taller the player is, the higher his salary is likely to be. (Sucks for most of us) Please refer to the top graph on the right. Typically the Center has the greatest height, hence the highest salary. All the rest follow in a similar order.
  • As for age, salary peaks around 30, which makes sense as it's the golden age of a player's career
  • For both effective field goals and total field goals, the most precise players do not get the highest salary. This is actually reasonable as the players who shoot the most accurate typically do not get in the game often.
  • Free throws, on the other hand, is a more straightforward indicator of salary, as most players get plenty of chances for freethrows.
